CPR Analysis Tool - tpldauftr Business Day Enhancement

CPR Executive Summary

Problem Statement

Current tpldauftr process archives closed orders without considering business day timing. Emergency runs during business hours archive same-day orders, making business "blind" to current operations.

Solution Overview

Enhanced business day logic with 6 AM cutoff time distinguishing between emergency (before 6 AM) and normal (after 6 AM) operations.

Key Requirements
  • Normal run (23:30): Archive same business day
  • Emergency run (8:00): Archive previous business day only
  • Weekend handling for business days
  • No impact on existing functionality
